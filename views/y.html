I am Vance Pope, born and raised in San Diego, Ca. I am a proud father of an 8-year-old boy named Emmet. I love to program, dance (or at least try to), play video games, hangout with friends and family.


My Time in the Navy

In June 2007, I decided to follow my father's footsteps and join the United States Navy. I shipped off to bootcamp later that year in October. After completing two years of schooling in Great Lakes, Illinois, I successfully became an Interior Communications Electrician (IC) and was due for assignment to the surface fleet. Upon arriving back home, I was stationed aboard the USS Germantown (LSD 42) as the ship's control console technician.
